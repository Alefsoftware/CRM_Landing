SELECT YOUR BANK ACCOUNT OR CREDIT CARD

Apael accepts the following payment methods:

  • Credit Card: Visa, MasterCard, Discover, American Express, JCB, Visa Electron. The total will be charged to your card when the order is shipped.
  • Apzel Fast Checkout: Allows you to securely save your credit card details so that you don't have to re-enter them for future purchases.
  • PayPal: Shop easily online without having to enter your credit card details on the website. Your account will be charged once the order is completed.
